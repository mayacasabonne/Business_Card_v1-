Video business card

Sixty seconds.

The whole of you.

Every colleague records a short spoken introduction so anyone can put a face and a voice to a name before the first meeting.

How it works

You answer three set questions and one you choose yourself. Each question appears on screen with its own timer. Recording runs straight through — no editing, no retakes mid-way. If you're not happy with it, record again from the start.

You'll need to allow camera and microphone access. Find a quiet spot with light on your face.
